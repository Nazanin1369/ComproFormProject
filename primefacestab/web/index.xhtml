Thank you for applying for the Master of Science in Computer Science Program. Since you have successfully registered with us, you can return to this page as many times as you need to complete all the required information.

The application has been divided into seven sections for your convenience and ease of use.

  • Start
  • Personal Data
  • English Proficiency
  • Education History
  • Professional Experience
  • Self Study
  • Select Program

As you provide your information to complete this application, you will be guided through the sections sequentially.

Each time you complete a section and click on the right arrow button your information will be saved.

When you have successfully completed all the sections, the Select Program tab will be activated. On this page you will be able to sign your application electronically and submit your application.

Important When you click the submit button, you will no longer be able to change the data. If your submitted application gets approved for phase 2, you will receive instructions on how to take the online programming test. Here is a link to the Application Steps.

The information you supply below will be kept confidential.

For information about the selection criteria, please see program requirements page. We kindly thank you for all your time and attention as you complete this process. Good luck to you!
